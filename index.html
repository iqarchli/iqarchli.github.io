Process

Initial Concept Phase

Generate low-fidelity sketches based on requirements received from PM. Present them to stakeholders to determine which ideas we want to pursue and which ones need further confirmation.

Concept Validation phase

Creating a high-fidelity prototypes of the chosen solution to evaluate its practicality and effectiveness through user testing. During this phase, I measure usability with metrics like the UMUX score, gathering quantitative and qualitative feedback. Based on the results, mock-ups are often refined to address any identified issues and improve the overall user experience.

Style Transfer

For this project, I worked on a Style Transfer tool that allows users to take a picture created with 3D tools and apply different artistic styles to it. Users can either choose from a library of preloaded styles or upload their own custom style to transform the image. This project was developed as part of a hackathon to explore how AI-driven style transfer could enhance the visual appeal of architectural renderings. The MVP focused on a smooth, intuitive user experience, with seamless image uploads and a real-time preview of applied styles, offering an innovative tool for designers and architects looking to experiment with new aesthetics.
